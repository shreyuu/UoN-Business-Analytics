# L7. SC Planning Methods & Techniques: MRP & Capacity Planning

## Learning Objectives

After studying this lecture, you should be able to:

- Define key **MRP concepts**, including **Materials Requirements Planning**, **Master Production Schedule (MPS)**, and **Bill of Materials (BOM)**
- Understand how **MRP supports planning and control** within operations and supply chain systems
- Apply **methods and techniques** used in **MRP**, including **time-phased requirements**, **lot-sizing**, and **capacity considerations**

- Read **Chapter 10** in _Bozarth & Handfield (2019)_
- Read **Chapter 8** in _Jacobs & Chase (2023)_
