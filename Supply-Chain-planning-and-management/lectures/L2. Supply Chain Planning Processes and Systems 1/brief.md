# L2. Supply Chain Planning Processes and Systems 1

## Learning Objectives

After studying this topic, you should be able to:

- Explain why we need operations and supply chain planning processes
- Describe the **Hierarchical Planning Model**
- Explain why we need to forecast to support supply chain and operations planning
- Explain the difference between **qualitative** and **quantitative** forecasting methods and be able to describe two qualitative forecasting approaches
- Explain the main reason why we hold inventory in operations and supply chain systems

### For the Self-Study Session, You Should Be Able To:

- Define **capacity** and explain the difference between **design capacity** and **effective capacity**
- Define **capacity utilisation**
