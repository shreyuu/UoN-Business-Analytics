# L6. SC Planning Methods & Techniques: Aggregate Planning

## Learning Objectives

After studying this lecture, you should be able to:

- Understand the **role and importance of aggregate planning** in supply chain planning and operations management
- Identify **when and how to apply different aggregate planning strategies** based on demand, capacity, and cost considerations
- Apply **aggregate planning techniques** and **interpret the outputs** of alternative strategies to support managerial decision-making

---

## Learning Activities

- Read **Chapter 8** of _Jacobs and Chase (2023)_
- Read **Chapter 10** of _Krajewski et al. (2018)_
- Read the journal article **“Sustainability in Supply Chain Management: Aggregate Planning from a Sustainability Perspective”**
