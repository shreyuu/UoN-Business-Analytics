# L4. SC Planning Methods & Techniques: Time Series Forecasting

## Learning Objectives

After studying this lecture, you should be able to:

- Explain the role and importance of forecasting in operations and decision-making
- Identify and describe key characteristics of time series data, including **trend**, **seasonality**, and **randomness**
- Apply appropriate forecasting methods for **stationary time series**, such as **moving averages** and **exponential smoothing**
- Evaluate forecasting performance using common accuracy measures (e.g. **MAD**, **MSE**, **MAPE**)
- Select and implement methods suitable for time series with **trends** and/or **seasonality**, including **trend-adjusted** and **seasonal models**
