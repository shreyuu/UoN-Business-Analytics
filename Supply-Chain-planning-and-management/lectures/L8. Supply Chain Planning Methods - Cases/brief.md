# L8. Supply Chain Planning Methods: Cases

## Learning Objectives

After studying this session, you should be able to:

- Analyse **real-world supply chain planning cases** using concepts and techniques covered in previous lectures
- Identify **planning challenges** and evaluate **alternative decision-making approaches** in different supply chain contexts
- Apply appropriate **supply chain planning methods** to propose and justify **practical solutions**
- Critically reflect on the **trade-offs and implications** of planning decisions across operational, tactical, and strategic levels

---

## Learning Activities

- Review all **case study materials** provided for this session
- Prepare to **participate in discussions** and apply planning tools to the cases
