# L5. SC Planning Methods & Techniques: Inventory Management

## Learning Objectives

After studying this lecture, you should be able to:

- Explain the **strategic role of inventory management** in operations, highlighting its impact on **cost efficiency**, **service levels**, and overall **supply chain performance**
- Identify and describe key **inventory characteristics**, including **demand patterns**, **lead time**, and **review periods**
- Identify major **inventory cost components**, such as **ordering**, **holding**, **shortage**, and **purchase costs**
- Apply **deterministic inventory models**, including **EOQ** and **POQ**, to determine optimal order quantities and minimise total inventory costs
- Analyse and implement the **stochastic (R, Q) model** to determine **reorder points**, **order quantities**, and **safety stock levels**
- Explain and calculate **service level measures**, including **cycle service level** and **fill rate**
- Evaluate trade-offs between **inventory policies** and **customer service levels** to design **cost-effective** and **responsive** inventory systems
